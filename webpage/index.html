Four and half million books

There are about four and a half million public domain books in the Hathi Trust. This is a zoomable visualization that lets you explore all of them. Click on any point to read the book in Hathi; zoom in to see more books in a region. Books are arranged in space using the LargeVis algorithm on their full-text; no metadata was consulted in arranging the library.
There are many clusters here besides the language ones that show up at the macro level. For instance, if you zoom in on the lower left tail of the English language books (just to the Northwest of the big French blob), you'll find a bunch of Shakespeare; zoom in even more, and you'll see that each of the Shakespeare plays occupies its own cluster.
There's no search yet for individual books. That's a complicated problem that would require using the Hathi API.
