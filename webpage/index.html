-close- Bias in word embeddings

This is a prototype plot of a way to visualize bias in large word embedding models, using 14 million reviews from RateMyProfessors. Words are colorized by how strongly they mirror two vectors in their model; one approximately "quality" or "goodness", the other distinguishing "male" from "female." Use the dropdown to switch views, zoom using the mouse or trackpad or whatever, or type a word into the search box to zoom directly to it.
